About Us

First Step Careers supports young people in taking their first step into employment by helping them build confidence, create strong CVs, prepare for interviews, and understand real career pathways. The aim is to make the transition from education into the workplace less intimidating and more accessible for young people who may not always know where to begin.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help young people feel confident and prepared when taking their first step into employment. By providing practical guidance on CV writing, interviews, job applications, and career pathways, First Step Careers aims to bridge the gap between education and the workplace.

School Workshops

First Step Careers delivers career readiness workshops for students from Year 10 and above. These sessions help young people build confidence, create strong CVs, prepare for interviews, and understand real career pathways into employment.
